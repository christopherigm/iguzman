Endpoints:
  GET  /health      – liveness check (no auth)
  POST /diarize     – speaker diarization only
  POST /transcribe  – speaker-attributed transcription

Authentication: pass your API key in the X-API-Key header.

First-boot note: On the initial pod start, pyannote and Whisper models are
downloaded from HuggingFace (~2–4 GB total). The startup probe allows up to
15 minutes for this. Subsequent restarts are instant if modelCache is enabled.

To enable the model cache PVC (avoids re-downloading on every restart):
  helm upgrade diarization ./helm --set modelCache.enabled=true

To check pod status:
  kubectl get pods -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} -l app.kubernetes.io/name=diarization

To tail logs:
  kubectl logs -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} -l app.kubernetes.io/name=diarization -f
